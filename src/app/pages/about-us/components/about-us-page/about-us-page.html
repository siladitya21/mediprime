Our Story

Building a Healthier Tomorrow

Mediprime Biotech Pvt. Ltd. is a fast-growing Indian pharmaceutical and nutraceutical company headquartered in Kolkata, committed to improving healthcare outcomes through evidence-based innovation, high-quality formulations, and ethical business practices.

Operating on a Pan-India scale, we specialize in scientifically backed products across probiotics, antibiotics with gut-health support, multivitamins, and appetite stimulants. Our portfolio includes Primolac (Pre-Probiotic formulations), the P-Cillin antibiotic series, Medfly 9G multivitamin, and Medicyp syrup.

What sets us apart is our commitment to clinical validation.
